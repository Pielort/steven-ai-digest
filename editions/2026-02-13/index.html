The AI Fragmentation-Convergence Paradox:

As AI capabilities become more democratized and accessible (convergence), nations and corporations are simultaneously creating proprietary, sovereign systems (fragmentation), leading to both fragmentation (different AI ecosystems) and convergence (similar capabilities across ecosystems). This paradox demands new approaches to creative production, technological independence, and economic planning in an increasingly AI-driven world.

The Autonomous Operations Layer:

February 2026 marks the transition from AI assistants to AI orchestrators. Agentic AI platforms can now execute complete business workflows autonomously, creating a new "Autonomous Operations Layer" that transforms enterprise productivity. Success requires rethinking workflows, upgrading infrastructure, and developing new human skills for the age of AI autonomy.
